Design a professional web application UI called "SI-EVASI" — 
an independent evaluation tool measuring understanding, technology acceptance 
(TAM), and usability (SUS) of a government financial application used by 
Police Department personnel in South Sulawesi, Indonesia.

---

## DESIGN PERSONALITY

- Official and trustworthy, but not stiff or intimidating
- Clean and easy to understand for non-technical users (police officers, 
  financial operators)
- Feels like a serious government professional system
- NOT a tech startup, NOT an AI product dashboard
- Forms feel light and not visually exhausting

Avoid: dark mode AI aesthetics, glassmorphism, neon accents, heavy animations, 
overly rounded corners, gradient meshes

---

## COLOR PALETTE

Primary Navy    #1B3A5C  → sidebar, header, primary buttons
Medium Blue     #2E6DA4  → active links, badges, active tabs
Light Gray BG   #F5F7FA  → main page background
Border Gray     #DDE3EC  → dividers, card borders
White           #FFFFFF  → card backgrounds, form inputs
Success Green   #2D9E6B  → success states, "Good/Very Good" category badges
Warning Amber   #E8A020  → warning states, "Fair" category badges
Danger Red      #C0392B  → errors, "Poor" category badges
Body Text       #1A2332  → all primary text
Secondary Text  #6B7A8D  → labels, placeholders, captions

---

## TYPOGRAPHY

Display/Heading font: Plus Jakarta Sans (Google Fonts)
Body/UI font: Inter (Google Fonts)
Base size: 14px body, 13px form labels, 22–28px page headings
No serif or decorative fonts
